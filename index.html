Led Zeppelin

Portrait of Led Zeppelin infront of their plane

In the city of London in the year 1968 four men

  • Jimmy Page
  • Robert Plant
  • John Paul Jones
  • John Bonham
came together to form possibly the greatest musical group of all time, Led Zeppelin. Arriving at their choice of name by referring to a jest made by a fellow musician, drummer for The Who, Keith Moon who said a band Jimmy Page was trying to start would go down like a "lead balloon" the band defied the odds and reached immense success through the 70s. Constantly experimenting and pushing the boundries of music until the tragic death of drummer John Bonham and their resulting break up.
